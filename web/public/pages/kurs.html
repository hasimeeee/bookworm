COURSES

A1 LEVEL ENGLISH COURSE

At A1 level, students learn basic greetings, introductions, and simple everyday phrases. They can form very basic sentences and express simple needs. They learn fundamental grammar rules and vocabulary.

Start the A1 Level Course

A2 LEVEL ENGLISH COURSE

At A2 level, students learn commonly used phrases and sentence patterns for daily interactions. They can talk about their backgrounds, surroundings, and simple communication needs. Basic reading and writing skills are developed.

Start the A2 Level Course

B1 LEVEL ENGLISH COURSE

At B1 level, students improve their ability to speak and write about familiar topics such as work, school, and leisure activities. They can express their feelings and thoughts in more detail. They can read and understand simple texts and write personal letters.

Start the B1 Level Course

B2 LEVEL ENGLISH COURSE

At B2 level, students can understand more complex texts and technical discussions. They can express their opinions and arguments in detail. They communicate fluently and naturally, with advanced written and spoken skills.

Start the B2 Level Course
